About Me

"I am a student of Systems Analysis and Development at FATEC in São José dos Campos - Professor Jessen Vidal. My focus is on back-end development, but I also have solid knowledge in front-end. I am improving my skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS through personal and academic projects.

Although I have no formal experience in technology positions, I applied my knowledge in the Integrated Learning Project (affectionately dubbed 'API') where I developed a Registration/Login system using Python, Flask, and MySQL. In addition, this project strengthened my communication, problem-solving, and time management skills, preparing me to work in agile development teams."

The proposal of this project was to create a personalized website for learning the SCRUM methodology, in a structured, organized, and personalized way. In addition, to provide an evaluation tool for users at the end of each module.
In this project we were able to develop skills and knowledge related to Front-End development with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS.
